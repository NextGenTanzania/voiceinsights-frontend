Public sample publications remain explicitly synthetic demonstrations and must never be presented as official statistics, real respondent findings, institutional certification or endorsement.

## Release acceptance
A publication is blocked when the sample is zero, findings or recommendations are missing, or evidence references cannot be resolved. Strong flagship samples expose executive, evidence, statistical, visual and decision intelligence and must achieve at least 90/100 to be export-eligible.
