Illustrative pilot scenario

Agriculture baseline survey — 1,200 respondents

A typical first pilot: an organization running a farmer support program needs a baseline before rollout. Using WhatsApp voice notes and phone calls across a rural region, the survey runs over two weeks instead of the six to eight weeks a paper-based baseline would typically take, with transcription and sentiment analysis available as responses arrive rather than after manual data entry.
