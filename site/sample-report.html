Key Findings

  • CRITICALCollateral requirements are the top-cited barrier to formal credit, mentioned by 61% of farmers.
  • HIGHMobile money adoption (89%) is far ahead of formal bank loan uptake (22%) — a gap, not a solved problem.
  • MEDIUMFarmers in a SACCOS/cooperative are markedly more likely to have received agricultural credit than those outside one.
AI Insight
Cooperative membership is the single strongest factor separating farmers with active credit from those without — stronger than region, age, or gender in this sample. This points to SACCOS expansion as a high-leverage intervention.

Recommendations

What should happen next.

Immediate (30 days)

  • Pilot a simplified collateral pathway for smallholder loans under a fixed threshold.
  • Promote existing mobile-money-linked micro-loan products more actively in surveyed districts.

Strategic (6–12 months)

  • Support SACCOS expansion in the two districts with lowest cooperative coverage.
  • Bundle financial literacy training with loan applications.

Policy (Long-term)

  • Advocate for a simplified land-title registration pathway for smallholder collateral use.
