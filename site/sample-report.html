Key Findings

  • Water scarcity is the top-cited issue, mentioned by 64% of respondents.
  • Women report a notably higher burden from water collection than men.
  • Coastal districts show the highest concentration of distress signals.
AI Insight
Fertilizer cost and water access together account for over half of all negative-sentiment responses — a strong signal for where intervention would have the most impact.

Recommendations

What should happen next.

Immediate (30 days)

  • Prioritize water point repair in the two coastal districts.
  • Share fertilizer price data with district agriculture offices.

Strategic (6–12 months)

  • Expand irrigation support to districts with lowest yield gains.
  • Launch a women-focused water access program.

Policy (Long-term)

  • Advocate for regional fertilizer subsidy review.
