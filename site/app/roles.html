Roles are fixed for now, not custom-configurable — this keeps permissions predictable and auditable. Support for custom roles (Client Viewer, Donor Viewer) is on the roadmap.

Super Admin

Maximum permissions. Currently held by 2 people.

  • Access every organization
  • Create organizations & projects
  • Invite users, assign to any organization/project
  • Suspend organizations
  • Manage billing, AI engines, integrations, security
  • Monitor fraud across the whole platform
  • View into (impersonate) any organization for support
Org Admin / M&E / Data Controller

Assigned by a Super Admin. Scoped to their own organization only.

  • Manage projects within their organization
  • Invite enumerators, assign to projects
  • Monitor collection progress
  • View reports, generate AI reports
  • Monitor data quality
  • Cannot manage billing
  • Cannot access other organizations
M&E Officer

A restricted variant of Org Admin — same organization, fewer settings.

  • View reports, respondents, interviews
  • View analytics and compliance status
  • Cannot manage billing or settings
  • Cannot invite users
Enumerator

The simplest interface — only what's needed to collect data.

  • See only their one assigned project
  • Collect interviews (offline-capable)
  • See their own today's/total count
  • No access to settings, billing, or other projects
