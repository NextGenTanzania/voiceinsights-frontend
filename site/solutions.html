Start with the decision workflow, not a list of features.

VoiceInsights combines collection, evidence governance, data products and action around four recurring institutional needs.

01 · Research & Evaluation

Produce defensible studies and decision-ready publications.

Buyer problem: fragmented field tools and manual publication work.

  1. Design and collect across appropriate channels
  2. Validate, process and analyze evidence
  3. Review findings and approve products
Roles
Research lead, M&E manager, data controller, enumerator
Outputs
Dataset, dashboard, technical report, publication, methodology, evidence pack
Governance
Versioned evidence, review status and traceability
Discuss research delivery →
02 · Monitoring & Performance

Connect programme indicators to review and action.

Buyer problem: reporting cycles that describe performance too late.

  1. Collect routine programme evidence
  2. Monitor indicators, quality and risks
  3. Publish periodic reports and track actions
Roles
Programme manager, M&E lead, organization administrator
Outputs
KPI dashboard, monitoring report, quarterly and annual publication
Governance
Approval gates, accountable owners and audit events
Discuss programme monitoring →
03 · Community Voice & Accountability

Turn feedback into a governed response loop.

Buyer problem: community input is collected but not visibly resolved.

  1. Open accessible multilingual channels
  2. Triage feedback and safeguarding signals
  3. Respond, report and close the loop
Roles
Accountability lead, safeguarding focal point, field team
Outputs
Feedback dashboard, issue dataset, scorecard, accountability report
Governance
Access controls, escalation and evidence custody
Discuss accountability workflows →
04 · Enterprise Decision Intelligence

Connect organizational evidence to executive decisions.

Buyer problem: data products remain separated from management action.

  1. Bring approved sources into context
  2. Analyze performance, risk and options
  3. Assign decisions and monitor resulting action
Roles
Executive, administrator, analyst, programme owner
Outputs
Executive dashboard, decision brief, board publication, action register
Governance
Role-aware access, provenance and decision auditability
