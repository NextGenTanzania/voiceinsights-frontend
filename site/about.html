About Us

Research infrastructure, built where the need is greatest.

VoiceInsights Africa exists because the languages and realities of emerging markets were never the priority for global research tools — so we built the tool ourselves.

Our Mission

To make voice-based research faster, cheaper, and higher-quality for organizations working in emerging and underserved markets — without cutting corners on the safety of the people behind the data.

Our Vision

A research operating system for Africa and other emerging markets — where any organization can collect, understand, and act on community voice, in the languages people actually speak.

Why Africa, why now

The languages global AI tools ignore are exactly where the need is.

Kiswahili and other African languages are dramatically under-represented in the training data behind most AI systems — yet these are the languages of the communities NGOs, governments, and researchers most need to hear from. Every interview VoiceInsights Africa processes builds a language asset that makes the next one better, in the markets that need it most.

Where we're based

Built in Dar es Salaam. Engineered for global research and impact teams.

VoiceInsights Africa serves organizations across Africa and global emerging markets through secure, compliant, multilingual voice intelligence infrastructure. Our headquarters are in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania — close to the field realities our platform is built to serve.
