Solutions

Built for real-world research workflows, not generic forms.

Whatever stage your program is at — from baseline to endline, from routine feedback to a crisis grievance line — VoiceInsights Africa adapts to how the work actually happens.

01

Survey Automation

Baseline and endline studies, market research, and rapid assessments — deployed across phone, WhatsApp, SMS, web, or offline field teams without redesigning your questionnaire for each channel.

02

Monitoring & Evaluation

Continuous tracking against your logframe indicators — weekly or monthly pulse surveys that feed straight into a live dashboard your donor can review directly.

03

Impact Evaluation

Before-and-after comparisons on program outcomes, with sentiment and topic analysis surfacing the "why" behind the numbers — not just the numbers themselves.

04

Beneficiary Feedback

Ongoing community voice channels that are simple enough for anyone to use — no literacy or smartphone required to be heard.

05

Grievance & Complaint Systems

Anonymous, safeguarding-first reporting channels with AI distress detection — built for humanitarian, gender-based violence, and accountability-to-affected-populations use cases.

06

Polling & Rapid Assessments

Fast-turnaround data collection for emergencies — a flood, a disease outbreak, a policy question that needs an answer this week, not next quarter.
