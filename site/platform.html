Platform

One pipeline — from a spoken answer to a decision-ready dataset.

VoiceInsights Africa is infrastructure, not a survey app: a layered system that captures voice at scale, understands it with AI, and returns structured, analyzable data your team can trust.

Architecture Overview

Five layers, working together end to end.

01

Collection Layer

Phone, WhatsApp, SMS, Web, and offline mobile — five channels writing into one respondent session model.

02

Storage Layer

Encrypted object storage for audio, a relational database for structured responses — access-controlled by role.

03

AI Processing Layer

Transcription, translation, classification, sentiment, and fraud detection run on every response automatically.

04

Analytics Layer

Real-time dashboards aggregate sentiment, topics, and response trends as data arrives — no batch delay.

05

Reporting Layer

Excel exports today; donor PDF and executive slide generation are on our near-term roadmap.

AI Engine

Six AI capabilities running on every single response.

Speech Recognition

Audio to text, tuned for Kiswahili, English, and natural code-switching between the two.

Summarization

Long interviews condensed into the findings that matter — without losing the respondent's own words.

Sentiment Detection

Positive, neutral, negative, and distress signals, tagged automatically at the moment of transcription.

Topic Extraction

Recurring themes surface across a campaign without anyone reading every transcript by hand.

Fraud Detection

Near-duplicate wording and suspiciously fast turnaround are flagged for review in real time.

Auto Reporting

Structured exports generated on demand — no manual data cleaning between collection and analysis.

Integrations

Built to fit into how your team already works.

VoiceInsights Africa runs on Cloudflare's global edge network with Twilio for telephony and WhatsApp delivery. Excel and CSV exports work with the tools your M&E team already uses. A documented API for custom integrations is on our roadmap — contact us if your organization needs one now.
