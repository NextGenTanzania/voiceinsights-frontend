# VoiceInsights International Report Standards v200

Every report should communicate: what happened, why it matters, what decision is required, what evidence supports it, and what action comes next.

Required visual pages include KPI command center, regional map intelligence, inclusion dashboard, sentiment drivers, maturity radar, risk heatmap, decision matrix, roadmap, impact forecast, SDG contribution wheel, evidence quality dashboard, board brief, donor VFM logic, government policy options and methodology annex.
