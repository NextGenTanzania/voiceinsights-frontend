# VoiceInsights Flagship Report Engine™ v2 — Phase 2 Premium Publications

Phase 2 converts the governed Phase 1 intelligence model into seven materially different publication systems:

1. Government Policy Intelligence
2. Donor Impact & Accountability
3. UN Evaluation & Country Intelligence
4. Executive Board Intelligence
5. Corporate & Market Intelligence
6. Technical Research & Statistical Publication
7. Humanitarian Needs & Severity Intelligence

Each style has its own cover personality, editorial tone, audience, mandatory section sequence, visualization system, international-standard emphasis and deliverable set. Styling cannot override the Phase 1 publication quality gate and cannot create missing evidence.

## New protected endpoints

- `GET /api/reports/flagship/premium/styles`
- `POST /api/reports/flagship/premium/compose`
- `POST /api/reports/flagship/premium/manifest`

## New workspace

- `/app/premium-publications.html`

## Acceptance principles

- Seven styles are visually and structurally distinct.
- The 16 flagship sample-report personalities map to one of the premium styles.
- Missing mandatory evidence remains marked `EVIDENCE_REQUIRED`.
- Every publication remains subject to the Phase 1 quality gate.
- Government, donor, UN, board, corporate, research and humanitarian products use audience-appropriate language and visual emphasis.
