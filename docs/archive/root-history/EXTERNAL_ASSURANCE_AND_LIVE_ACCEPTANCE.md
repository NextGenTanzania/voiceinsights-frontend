# External Assurance & Live Acceptance Pack

This release adds the execution harness, evidence registry and acceptance records needed to complete international assurance. It does **not** fabricate external tests or certifications.

## What is deployable now
- SSO live-test plans for Microsoft Entra ID, Google Workspace and Okta.
- SCIM create/update/suspend/restore lifecycle validator.
- MFA recovery and privileged-action challenge evaluator.
- External penetration-test evidence register.
- SOC 2 and ISO external-review evidence register.
- Real-client Demo → Founder Approval → Organization → Project → Campaign acceptance run.
- Founder-controlled evidence recording and audit-safe metadata redaction.

## What still requires third parties or real accounts
- Provider credentials and test tenants for Entra ID, Google Workspace or Okta.
- A real SCIM-capable identity provider.
- Real privileged user accounts for MFA recovery testing.
- An independent penetration-testing firm.
- An independent SOC 2 auditor and ISO certification body.
- A consenting real pilot client.

No production or certification claim should be made until these executions are completed and their evidence references are stored.
