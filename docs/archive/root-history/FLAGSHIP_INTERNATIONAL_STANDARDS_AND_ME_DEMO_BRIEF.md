# VoiceInsights Flagship International Standards & M&E Demo Brief Release

This release strengthens all sixteen flagship synthetic demonstration reports with explicit, evidence-aware mappings for:

- UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)
- OECD-DAC evaluation criteria
- Results-Based Management (RBM) and Theory of Change
- Core Humanitarian Standard (CHS)
- UN evaluation principles where contextually applicable
- Data protection, responsible AI and responsible-use disclosures

The mappings demonstrate reporting architecture and do not claim certification or endorsement by the referenced institutions.

## Branded publication outputs

The interactive report viewer now includes the VoiceInsights Africa logo and name on the cover. PDF outputs include a vector VoiceInsights brand mark and wordmark, while Word, PowerPoint and Excel outputs carry the prepared-by identity and international framework sections.

## Separate M&E Demo Brief

The M&E Demo Workspace now downloads a separate PDF titled **M&E Evidence, Quality & Decision Brief**. It is not one of the sixteen flagship reports. It is clearly marked as synthetic demonstration content and includes standards alignment, evidence, quality, decision intelligence and management actions.

Public routes:

- `GET /api/public/demo/me-brief`
- `GET /api/public/demo/me-brief/export/pdf`
